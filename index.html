About

I came up in sales. Then I started building the systems sales actually needs.

I spent years in full-cycle B2B sales. Sourcing, running discovery, handling objections, closing. I know what an SDR and AE's day actually looks like. I know the moment in a demo or meeting when conversion either lands or slips.

That experience is the part most GTM tools are missing. Most are built by engineers who have never sat on a call. The output is technically correct and commercially useless. Generic personalisation, dashboards no one opens, qualification that wastes credits before it filters anything.

So I learned to build. Clay, Python, Playwright, n8n, Claude Code, React Native, Next.js. Now I ship GTM systems where the engineering reflects how deals actually move, not how a vendor says they should.

That's the angle. Seller's brain. Builder's hands. Both, not one.
